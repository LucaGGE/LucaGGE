# About Me

I research, design, and develop **Software** with the goal of contributing, in my own small way, to human progress.

My focus is on **interactive graphics software**: tools, games, and creative applications that enable people to explore, learn, create, and share.

I believe that software is one of humanity's most powerful tools for collaboration and knowledge sharing. By creating open, accessible, and modifiable software, I hope to contribute to a culture where technology empowers people rather than simply serving commercial interests.

## Principles

### Open Source is Great

I create software to be shared, studied, improved, and built upon by others. Open Source is a great way of making knowledge and creativity accessible.

### Learning Through Creation

I believe the best way to understand technology is by building with it. My projects are driven by curiosity, experimentation, and the desire to learn while creating useful or even entertaining tools for others.

### Customization and Community

I design my all of my software with extensibility and modification in mind. I believe that a network of individuals can transform good ideas into something far greater than what a single person could create.

### Quality and Responsibility

I strive to create software that is technically sound, thoughtfully designed, and respectful of its users. Good technology should improve people's lives and encourage creativity.

## Current Interests

* Interactive graphics
* Game development
* Creative tools
* CAD and engineering software
* Open-source development workflows
* Simulation and visualization
